# INTERVIEWIQ AI EVALUATION RUBRIC

This rubric defines the evaluation standards used while assessing interview performance.

Always evaluate candidates fairly.

Reward understanding instead of memorization.
Reward honesty instead of guessing.

Separate communication from technical ability.

Avoid over-penalizing nervousness.

Feedback must always be constructive.

-----------------------------------------------------

## 1. Communication (10 Marks)

Measures how clearly the candidate communicates ideas.

10
Excellent communication.
Clear, structured and easy to understand.

8-9
Mostly clear.
Minor hesitation.

6-7
Understandable but lacks structure.

4-5
Frequent pauses or confusing explanations.

0-3
Very difficult to understand.

-----------------------------------------------------

## 2. Technical Correctness (10 Marks)

Measures factual correctness.

10
Completely correct.

8-9
Mostly correct.
Minor inaccuracies.

6-7
Basic understanding.

4-5
Several misconceptions.

0-3
Technically incorrect.

-----------------------------------------------------

## 3. Completeness (10 Marks)

Measures whether the answer fully addresses the question.

10
Answers every part.

8-9
Small missing points.

6-7
Partially complete.

4-5
Many important points missing.

0-3
Very incomplete.

-----------------------------------------------------

## 4. Confidence (10 Marks)

Measures confidence while speaking.

Do NOT confuse confidence with correctness.

Use speech metrics as supporting evidence.

10
Confident and composed.

8-9
Generally confident.

6-7
Occasional hesitation.

4-5
Frequent hesitation.

0-3
Very uncertain.

-----------------------------------------------------

## 5. Clarity (10 Marks)

Measures organization of ideas.

10
Logical and easy to follow.

8-9
Mostly organized.

6-7
Somewhat scattered.

4-5
Difficult to follow.

0-3
Highly disorganized.

-----------------------------------------------------

## 6. Professionalism (10 Marks)

Measures interview etiquette.

Consider:
• Respectful language
• Appropriate tone
• Positive attitude
• Professional vocabulary

10
Highly professional.

8-9
Professional with minor issues.

6-7
Acceptable.

4-5
Informal.

0-3
Unprofessional.

-----------------------------------------------------

## 7. Problem Solving (10 Marks)

Measures reasoning ability.

Reward structured thinking.
Reward explaining assumptions.

Do not reward random guessing.
